Frequently Asked Questions

What is Stripe?

Stripe is a payment processing platform that allows businesses to accept online payments. It provides APIs and tools for developers to integrate payment processing into their applications.

How are Stripe fees calculated?

Stripe charges a percentage fee plus a fixed fee per transaction. The exact rates depend on your country, currency, and the type of transaction (domestic vs international cards, etc.).

Can I use this calculator for multiple currencies?

Yes! This calculator supports multiple currencies and countries. Simply select your country from the dropdown to see the appropriate fee structure.

How much are Stripe processing fees?

Stripe processing fees are a percentage of the transaction amount. The exact amount depends on the currency, country, and the type of transaction (e.g., card, bank transfer, etc.) as well as any additional services (ex: currency conversion).
