Manan Patel is a Senior Software Engineer and Entrepreneur based in Calgary, Alberta, Canada.

- **Current:** Business Consultant at Rocket Agency (Aug 2025–Present): consults startups on remote staffing, technical automations, AI workflows; placed 25+ full-time offshore staff (LATAM/Africa/PH) in 6 months.
- **Recent:** Co-Founder & CTO at RentPlay (Jan–Sep 2025): OCR-based shop-local rewards, React Native/Node/TypeScript; raised $100K SAFE, $50K grants (Alberta Innovates, SR&ED); returned 90% of investor capital.
- **Background:** Team Lead & Senior iOS Developer at Environment and Climate Change Canada (Mar 2023–Dec 2024): WeatherCan iOS app, 5M+ monthly users, Canada’s #3 weather app; GPS severe weather alerts, Radar (Lightning, Hurricane, Tornado). Senior iOS Engineer at Wattpad (Aug 2021–Mar 2023): SwiftUI Comments for 90M+ users. iOS Developer at TD Bank (Jan 2020–Aug 2021): TD App, biometric auth, PCI-DSS. iOS/Software Developer at ECCC (May 2017–Jan 2020): WeatherCAN launch, REST APIs, VueJS for Weather.gc.ca.
- **Education:** BSc Computer Science & Statistical Machine Learning, University of Toronto (2014–2019). Coursework: statistical ML, neural networks, deep learning, NLP, computer vision, multivariate analysis.
- **Skills:** Swift, SwiftUI, UIKit, React Native, Node.js, TypeScript, Python; iOS (Core Data, CloudKit, StoreKit, MapKit); backend (REST, GraphQL, Firebase); ML (TensorFlow, PyTorch); Agile, TDD, product management.
- **Projects:** Goal Getter (habit tracking, SwiftUI, CloudKit, 1K+ MAU, paying subscribers). Countries Quiz (SwiftUI, MapKit, 500+ MAU).
- **Interests:** AI, AR, algo trading, CPG, lifestyle businesses, impactful charities. Fitness, hiking, pickleball, film (Letterboxd), travel.
